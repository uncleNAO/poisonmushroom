Eyeballz Cube, 2018

Eyeballz Cube is a modular kinetic installation. Its modular design makes it adaptable to diverse environments and spaces by various layout and combinations.

Each eyeball cube moves independently and randomly, just like the attention of each one of us, directed at different things at any given moment. When many eyeball cubes are stacked together, their quick and chaotic movement shows the beauty of disorder, just like the hustle and bustle of our society.

眼球立方是一个模块化的动态艺术装置，模块化的设计使得它能以灵活的排列组合占据不同类型的空间。每个眼球立方独立而随机地运动，正如我们每个人的注意力随时随地被不同事物所吸引。当许许多多个眼球立方层叠在一起时，它们快速而稍显混乱的运动呈现了一种无序的美感，正如我们充斥着喧嚣与繁杂的社会。

Eyeballz Cube can be made interactive by adding external cameras and custom software that tracks body movement. The eyeballs will follow people as they go, as if they are closely watching.

眼球立方也可以拓展成具有交互功能的动态装置。通过配备摄像头和定制软件，眼球立方可以随着人体的运动而运动，形成“一直在看你”的效果。
