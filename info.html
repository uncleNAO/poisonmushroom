Poison Mushroom is an artistic collective dedicated to developing novel and playful interactions and experiences. Unlike the poison mushroom in Super Mario Bros, you will not shrink when you come across our work, but you might get pleasantly-surprised and weirdly-intrigued.

Poison Mushroom is based in Shenzhen, China.

毒蘑菇(Poison Mushroom) 是致力于创造好玩的交互方式和互动体验的艺术团队。 跟超级马里奥兄弟里面的“毒蘑菇”不同， 我们的作品不会使你身体缩小，不过你也许会感到意外的惊喜和莫名的好奇。

毒蘑菇位于中国深圳。

Poison Mushroom is founded by D.Z. Xia and Wei Li in 2017. D.Z. Xia holds a Master’s degree in Interaction Design from Umea Institute of Design and has worked as interaction designer & engineer in companies such as Microsoft, Philips and IDEO. Wei Li is a graduate from the Master’s Design Program at Stanford University. She was Artist / researcher-in-residence at Autodesk Pier9 and specialized in the use of digital fabrication tools in creating new forms and aesthetics. Her work has been featured on Wired, PSFK, The Creators Project, Designboom, Smithsonian, to name a few.

毒蘑菇于2017年由夏冬至和李唯创立。 夏冬至毕业于于默奥大学交互设计硕士专业，并在微软、飞利浦、IDEO等公司担任过交互设计师/工程师，具有丰富的行业经验。 李唯毕业于斯坦福大学设计硕士专业， 并在Autodesk Pier9 机构担任过驻地艺术家/研究员， 主要探索新型数字制造工具在创造新的艺术形式和科技美学中的应用。 她的作品曾被Wired, PSFK, The Creators Project, Designboom, Smithsonian等媒体报道。
